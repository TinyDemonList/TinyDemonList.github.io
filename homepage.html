The Tiny Demon List

Site made by ScratchMan92

List made by Epic GD

A list completely dedicated to tiny levels.

Record Submission Rules (List Rules)

  • Completions must include clicks.
  • 2 Player levels need to have been beaten solo to be accepted.
  • Any progresses on levels count as long as it's past the given percentage. (Main List)
  • Do not submit multiple records of the same completion.
  • No noclip of any sort.

Other List Rules

  • All levels on this list need to be tiny. (Under 10 seconds)
  • All levels positions are based off of the opinions of list mods in the discord.
  • Levels cannot include any cheese or secret ways, levels that have any will instantly be removed.
